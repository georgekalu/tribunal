Proposing the Motion

Killers Are Born

"Ladies and gentlemen, I stand firmly in support of this motion."

01
The Science of the Dark Triad
›
Research consistently identifies a cluster of personality traits — psychopathy, narcissism, and Machiavellianism — that are significantly heritable. Psychopaths, making up roughly 1% of the population, show reduced activity in the amygdala, the brain's fear and empathy centre. This is not learned. It is wired. A true killer does not need to be trained to suppress conscience — they were born without the full burden of one.
02
Soldiers Require External Architecture
›
Every army in history has understood that ordinary men do not kill willingly. Military training must manufacture the will to kill through dehumanisation, obedience, and desensitisation. Lt. Colonel Dave Grossman's On Killing reveals that most WWII soldiers deliberately fired over the enemy's heads. A born killer needs no such re-engineering.
03
Serial Killers vs. Combat Veterans
›
Thousands of combat veterans return home and never harm another person. Yet serial killers — Bundy, Dahmer, Chikatilo — many with no military experience, report their compulsion as present since childhood. None were soldiers. All were killers before they ever acted. The impulse predated the act.
🛡️
Opposing the Motion

Killers Are Made

"Ladies and gentlemen, this motion is not only flawed — it is dangerous."

01
It Absolves Society of Responsibility
›
If killers are simply "born," we need not examine poverty, trauma, or abuse. ACE studies show a direct link between early trauma and later violence. Most people on death row share histories of severe neglect and abandonment. They were not born killers — they were built into them by the world that failed them.
02
Killing Goes Against Human Nature
›
If soldiers must be made — trained, conditioned, reshaped — then killing is not a natural default. Jonathan Haidt and other moral psychologists argue that moral intuition against harming others is among the most universal human traits. Killing goes against our grain — which is exactly why it must be trained into people.
03
The Neuroscience of Environment
›
Brain structure is not fixed at birth. Neuroplasticity means sustained exposure to violence physically reshapes the brain. Child soldiers in Uganda, radicalised teenagers in Europe, gang members in urban warzones — none were born killers. They were harvested by circumstance.
04
The "Born Killer" Label Is a Social Weapon
›
Throughout history, calling a group "born killers" has justified genocide, mass incarceration, and dehumanisation. It strips agency and denies rehabilitation. Science, ethics, and justice all demand we reject this framing.

✦ Final Verdict ✦

Soldiers are made. And so, tragically, are killers — by neglect, violence, ideology, and the failures of the societies that shaped them.
